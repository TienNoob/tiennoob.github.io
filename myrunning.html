FAQ - You Asked. We Answered!

Find answers to frequently asked questions about running, shoes and more.

🧑‍💻
👟 Running & Performance
🏃
How can I improve my running pace?
⌄
Focus on interval training, strength workouts, and gradually increasing your weekly mileage. Consistency is key — run 3–4 times per week and incorporate speed work once a week.
What is the best way to warm up before a run?
⌄
Start with 5–10 minutes of brisk walking or light jogging, then do dynamic stretches like leg swings, hip circles, and high knees. Avoid static stretching before a run.
How often should I be running each week?
⌄
Beginners should aim for 3 runs per week with rest days. Intermediate runners can target 4–5 days. Always include at least one full rest day.
👟 Shoe Maintenance
How do I clean my running shoes properly?
›
Remove insoles and laces, brush off loose dirt, then use a damp cloth with mild soap. Air dry away from direct heat.
When should I replace my running shoes?
›
Most running shoes last 500–800 km. Replace when you notice increased soreness or worn-down outsoles.
How often should I be running each week?
›
3–5 days per week is generally recommended with adequate rest and recovery days.
📱 Running App & Tracking
How accurate is the My Running app in tracking distance?
›
The app uses GPS and is accurate to within 2–5% of actual distance in open environments.
Can I sync My Running app with other fitness apps/devices?
›
Yes! My Running supports syncing with Apple Health, Google Fit, Garmin, and Strava. Go to Settings › Connections.
